  • Message Content: All private and group chats are End-to-End Encrypted (E2EE). We have no technical ability to decrypt them.

  • Location History: We do not track your movements. Location sharing is encrypted and temporary.

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell your personal data. We only share it with technical providers necessary for the operation of the service:

  • Hosting: Reek.cz (Server Infrastructure). For providers outside the EU, we ensure protection using Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC).

We use state-of-the-art technology to protect your data:

  • Infrastructure: Protection against attacks, rate limiting, and minimization of stored data.
