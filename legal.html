Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 6, 2025

At Clyo, privacy isn't just a feature; it's the foundation. We collect the absolute minimum amount of data required to make the app function.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Message Content: All 1:1 chats, group chats, and calls are End-to-End Encrypted. We literally cannot decrypt them.
  • Media Files: Photos and large files (up to 50GB) are encrypted on your device before uploading. They are stored temporarily for delivery and then permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Location History: We do not log your movements. Live Location is fleeting and encrypted.

2. Data We Do Collect

To operate the service, we must process minimal metadata:

  • Account Info: Your username, public keys (for encryption), and profile picture (if set).
  • Connection Data: Temporary IP logs for rate-limiting and preventing DDOS attacks. These are rotated and wiped regularly.
  • Contacts: If you enable contact syncing, we use a privacy-preserving hash matching to find friends. Your raw address book is never stored on our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Spotify Integration: If you choose to link Spotify, we display your "Currently Playing" status to your contacts. This data is fetched directly from Spotify's API and is not stored by Clyo.

Push Notifications: To notify you of new messages when the app is closed, we use standard push services (Apple APNs / Google FCM). The content of the notification is encrypted.

GDPR Compliance

If you are a user in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have specific rights regarding your personal data.

1. Your Rights

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of your account data. (Note: Since we don't store message history, this will mostly be account metadata).
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can delete your account at any time within the app. This permanently wipes your cryptographic keys from our directory, making any undelivered messages unreadable.
  • Right to Portability: You can export your locally stored chat history from the app settings.

2. Data Controller

Clyo is developed by the Clyo Team.

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact us at: privacy@clyo.online (Placeholder).

3. International Transfers

We use servers located in secure data centers. We ensure that any data transfer complies with EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).
